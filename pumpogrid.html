The Nodes
How to interact
TAP / CLICK
Send a pulse of energy outward from that point. Nearby nodes absorb the pulse and accelerate their growth. The further away, the weaker the effect.
HOLD
Sustain a pulse at a fixed point. Useful for nurturing a specific cluster or stabilising a red instability node. Drains your active energy faster.
DRAG
Draw an energy line between two nodes to attempt a direct connection. If both nodes are compatible, a permanent conduit forms between them.
SCROLL / PINCH
Zoom in and out on your empire. Pan by dragging on empty space. Your empire is larger than your current view — zoom out to explore.
