HIDDEN COORDINATION
A Game Theory Survival Simulation
How it works
01
Each round, 25 agents (including you) broadcast one of four signals — Alpha, Beta, Gamma, or Delta.
02
Agents in the largest cluster survive. Agents in weaker clusters face elimination. The fewer agents remain, the higher the pressure.
03
Agents remember what worked. Watch the Field Intelligence panel — signal win history tells you where the field is coordinating.
04
Be the last agent standing. In Ghost Match mode, your session is recorded and others face your field.
The meta
You cannot communicate. You cannot see others' choices. You can only read the field's history and guess where the crowd will go — then go there first. This is how coordination works in the real world.
